The Essentials





The key essentials every plant asks for




🌞 Light

Keep your indoor plants in bright indirect sunlight for healthier leaves and faster growth.

💧 Water

Water your plants only when the top layer of soil becomes dry to avoid root damage.

🌡️ Warmth

Most indoor plants prefer warm temperatures and stable humidity throughout the day.
